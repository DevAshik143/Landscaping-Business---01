Frequently Asked Questions

What are the best plants for my garden?

Discover a variety of plants suitable for your garden, including flowers, shrubs, trees, and vegetables, considering factors like climate, soil type, sunlight, and space availability.

How do I prepare my soil for planting?

Learn the essential steps for soil preparation, including testing, amending, and conditioning to create a healthy and fertile environment for your plants to thrive.

What are the best watering techniques for my garden?

Explore different watering methods such as drip irrigation, soaker hoses, and hand watering, and understand how to adjust frequency and duration based on plant needs and weather conditions.

How can I control pests in my garden naturally?

Discover eco-friendly pest control solutions using companion planting, beneficial insects, organic sprays, and physical barriers to protect your plants from common pests and diseases.

When and how should I prune my plants?

Learn the importance of proper pruning timing and techniques for maintaining plant health, promoting growth, shaping, and rejuvenating, with tips for different types of plants and pruning tools.
