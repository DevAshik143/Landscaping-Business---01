Gardening Workshops

Join us for an enlightening Gardening Workshop and expand your knowledge on plant care, landscaping, and sustainable gardening practices.

Explore the art and science of gardening at our workshop. The Gardening Workshop aims to educate participants on various gardening techniques, plant selection, soil management, and eco-friendly gardening practices.

Event Agenda:

Join us for the Gardening Workshop with the following agenda:

  • Introduction to Basic Gardening Techniques.
  • Plant Selection and Landscape Design Tips.
  • Soil Management and Composting Methods.
  • Water Conservation Strategies for Gardeners.
  • Interactive Demonstrations and Hands-on Activities.
